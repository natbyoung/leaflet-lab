Intercultural Identity:

As more cultures meet and intermingle in the "Great American Melting Pot," it stands to reason that more Americans will identify as multi-racial. This map explores that idea, showing city population percentages of those that identify as multi-racial year over year from 2010 and 2016.

Information shown may also be filtered by total multi-racial population in each city via the filter in the top right of the map. The user may also track a specific city by clicking it and tracking its data in the panel above.
