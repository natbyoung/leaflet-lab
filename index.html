Explore Population Changes:

Total population from 1970 to 2020 is mapped for all cities in Washington with populations of at least 50,000 in 2020. Select the year using the slider.

The mapped cities can be adjusted using the filter menu of specific total population ranges. Click a city to view additional information in the information panel, which will appear beside or below the map.

Data Source: IPUMS NHGIS, University of Minnesota, www.nhgis.org (Time Series Table AVO).
All original source datasets of table AVO are from the U.S. Census Bureau.

Data Notes: Data are decennial census data with the exception of year 2019, which represents 2015-2019 American Community Survey Data.
Some places did not exist in the earlier years of this dataset. The population for these places and years has been set to zero (original data is null).
Geographic Integration: Nominally integrated tables. Geographic units are linked across time according to their names and codes, disregarding any changes in unit boundaries.
